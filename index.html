Welcome to Homes for Davenport

Advocating for affordable housing in the Davenport neighborhood of Toronto.

Our Mission

Our mission is to advocate for the construction of affordable housing in Davenport and around Toronto, where everyone should have access to a safe and affordable home.

Our Strategy

At Homes for Davenport we work with neighbors, local organizations, and policymakers to develop sustainable housing solutions. This work includes education about affordable housing and direct advocacy for specific projects in Davenport.

Affordable Housing

What is affordable housing?
Affordable housing is defined as housing that costs 30% of less of a household's before-tax income. About half of Toronto's renters spend above this amount, and would benefit from more affordable housing builds.

Who is responsible?
In Ontario, cities are responsible for providing affordable housing.

How can we help?
Engaging with stakeholders, especially neighbors, can lead to real effective change when it comes to affordable housing in Toronto and Davenport.
